Skye's Portfolio

Step 1: Sign up for a GitHub account
Step 2: Sign in to your GitHub account
Step 3: Click on your profile picture and choose "Your repositories"
Step 4: Clean the "New" button next to the "Find a repository" search bar
Step 5: Input your "Repository name", for example: "yourUsername.github.io", and click the "Create repository" button
Step 6: Click your profile picture again, and you will see the newly created repository.
Step 7: Click on "yourUsername.github.io", now you are able to upload existing files (if you have any) or create new ones for your repository.

First GitHub is now setting up.
